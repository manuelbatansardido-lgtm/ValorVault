About ValorVault

Learn more about our mission and the app

Our Mission

ValorVault was created with a simple goal: to help Mobile Legends players earn in-game diamonds without spending real money. We believe everyone should have access to premium game content, regardless of their financial situation.

Through our rewards platform, users can complete simple tasks like watching ads, spinning wheels, and solving captchas to earn valors – our in-app currency – which can then be redeemed for real Mobile Legends diamonds.

About The App

ValorVault is a free Android rewards application designed specifically for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang players. Our app provides multiple ways to earn virtual currency (valors) that can be converted to in-game diamonds.

How You Earn

ValorVault offers multiple earning methods to suit different preferences:

Watch Ads

Earn valors by watching short rewarded video advertisements. Each ad takes about 15-30 seconds and rewards you with valors instantly.

Daily Login

Claim daily rewards just for opening the app! Build a login streak to earn bonus valors. The longer your streak, the better the rewards.

Spin Wheel

Test your luck with our spin wheel! Win between 1-15 valors per spin. You get multiple free spins daily.

Solve Captchas

Complete simple math captchas to earn valors. Quick, easy, and helps keep our platform secure from bots.

Idle Miner

Start the idle miner and earn valors passively even when you're not using the app. Perfect for earning while you sleep!

Wallet System

Send valors to friends! Once you activate your wallet, you can transfer your earnings to other users.

Redemption System

Once you've accumulated enough valors, you can redeem them for Mobile Legends diamonds. Here are our current packages:

Redemption requests are processed manually and typically take 24-48 hours.

Security & Trust

Your safety is our priority. Here's how we keep your account secure:

  • Google Sign-In: We use secure Google authentication – we never see your password.
  • Firebase Security: All data is stored securely on Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption.
  • No Password Required: We never ask for your Mobile Legends password. We only need your Player ID and Server ID to deliver diamonds.
  • Anti-Fraud System: Our app includes measures to prevent abuse and ensure fair rewards for all users.
