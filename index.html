Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Is ValorVault free to use?

Yes! ValorVault is completely free to download and use. You earn valors by completing simple tasks - no purchase required.

How long does it take to get diamonds?

Once you redeem your valors for diamonds, the processing typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification when your diamonds are delivered.

Is my account safe?

Absolutely! We use secure Google Sign-In authentication and never ask for your Mobile Legends password. Your data is protected with Firebase security.

What is the wallet feature?

The wallet allows you to send valors to other users! Activate your wallet for 100 valors, then you can transfer valors to friends. Note: A 20% fee applies to transfers.

How many tasks can I complete daily?

You can watch unlimited ads, complete up to 50 captchas, and spin the wheel 3 times daily. Daily login rewards reset at midnight. Idle mining runs continuously!
